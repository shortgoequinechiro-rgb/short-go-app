Powerful Features Built for Your Practice

Everything you need to run a modern animal chiropractic practice, from scheduling to advanced diagnostics.

Patient Records

Complete equine and canine profiles with breed, discipline, medical history, and visit tracking. Build a comprehensive patient database that grows with your practice.

Spine Assessment

Species-specific spinal maps. Track subluxation findings visit-to-visit and visualize patient progress. Standardized assessments for consistent care.

AI SOAP Notes

Generate professional SOAP notes from quick clinical observations. One click, done. Save hours on documentation while maintaining clinical accuracy.

Scheduling

Book appointments, send confirmations, and automate reminders via email and SMS. Reduce no-shows and keep your practice running smoothly.

Digital Forms

Send intake and consent forms directly to clients. E-signatures. No paper. Clients sign on their phone, you get records instantly.

Offline Mode

Works without cell signal. Take notes in the barn, the arena, or the pasture. Everything syncs automatically when you're back online.
